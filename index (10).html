Our Story

Where the island meets artistry

Happy Nails is a relaxed, welcoming spot for manicures, pedicures, and brow waxing with friendly staff. Since opening on Hilton Head Island, we've built a loyal community of clients who love the care and skill our technicians bring to every appointment.

Our team — including skilled technicians Sky, Amber, and Jackie — are attentive to detail and dedicated to making every visit personal. We welcome walk-ins, accept all major credit cards, and our salon is fully wheelchair accessible with a restroom available.
